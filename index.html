ABOUT

I ♥ BETHNAL GREEN is the online home of SAVE OUR SAFER STREETS, a group of Bethnal Green residents who want to keep and improve the newly built pedestrian-friendly streets around Old Bethnal Green Road, Columbia Road and Arnold Circus.

Our much-loved new, safe streets and community spaces are being threatened with removal by Tower Hamlets Council, who have launched a consultation to take out the £2 million of changes which were implemented in 2021.

Beyond the immediate campaign to save our new streets, parklets and community spaces, we will encourage the council to engage with all the residents in Bethnal Green to explore ways to make our public spaces better. Everyone wants to improve air quality and create safer streets for people to walk, talk and play on. We believe this is an ongoing experiment that we want everyone to feel they can benefit from.

ACTION

Tower Hamlets Council
STOP, LISTEN and IMPROVE

If you live, work or study in Tower Hamlets, please read and sign our petition to Tower Hamlets Council.

We are asking the Council to:

  • STOP plans to scrap the new street layouts
  • LISTEN to the diverse communities and stakeholders in our borough
  • IMPROVE the new street layouts to keep what is working and improve where needed

We want our streets to be improved so that everyone’s health, wellbeing and safety gets better.

Please consider writing to the Tower Hamlets Mayor and your local councillors. Tell them how the new street layouts have improved your quality of life and that you want them improved not removed.
