About I ♥ Bethnal Green

I ♥ Bethnal Green is an action group made up of residents who want to keep and improve the Liveable Streets programme in Bethnal Green.

In 2022, in the local elections, a new mayor and a number of new council members were elected in Tower Hamlets. The mayor and some of these councillors pledged to reverse the Tower Hamlets Liveable Streets programme that was designed to reduce pollution in the borough and increase the green spaces many enjoy. I ♥ Bethnal Green is campaigning to stop this reversal and find beneficial ways forward for everyone.

Beyond the immediate campaign to save the Liveable Streets programme, I ♥ Bethnal Green wants to engage all the residents in Bethnal Green to improve the environment we live in. We want to explore all the options we can to improve the quality of air for everyone and create safer streets for people to walk, talk and play on. We believe this is an ongoing experiment that we want everyone to feel they can benefit from.

ACTION POINT

Complete the Tower Hamlets Council survey by 31 July!

If you were sent a consultation pack this week make sure you use the code that was included in it.

You can send a response either via post or complete the survey online (there are two surveys, one for Weavers and one for Old Bethnal Green Road).

The only way we are going to build a cleaner and safer environment in Bethnal Green is to show how many of us want this. Please take this one action!

In addition, please consider writing to the Tower Hamlets Mayor and your local councillors. Also let your friends and neighbours know what is going on and if they don't want Liveable Streets to be reversed, ask them to complete the consultation too.
