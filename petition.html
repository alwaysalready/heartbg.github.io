If you live, work or study in Tower Hamlets, please read and sign our petition to Tower Hamlets Council.

We are asking the Council to STOP plans to scrap the new street layouts, LISTEN to the diverse communities and stakeholders in our borough and IMPROVE the new street layouts to keep what is working and improve where needed.

We want our streets to be improved so that everyone’s health, wellbeing and safety gets better. Read and sign the petition here. You don't need to register - just click ‘sign petition’, fill in your personal info, and ‘continue’ and ‘submit’.
