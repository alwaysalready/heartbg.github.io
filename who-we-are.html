 WHO WE ARE 

Wondering who’s behind the Save Our Safer Streets campaign?

We’re a group of locals in the Bethnal Green, Columbia Road and Arnold Circus area. We came together in July 2022 when Tower Hamlets started a consultation to rip out the new traffic calming measures in our local area. Some of us are neighbours and knew each other from before, and some met everyone for the first time at one of the local meet-ups we held throughout July and August. We are all volunteers and we are doing what we can to raise awareness about how the new road layouts have benefitted the community and about the council's plans to rip them out, working around full-time jobs and family commitments. As you can imagine, we can always use more help, so please get in touch if you have a bit of time to spare! We’d love to have a chat.
